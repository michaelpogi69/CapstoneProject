Baguio City Overview

Welcome to Baguio City

Baguio City is the Philippines’ premier mountain destination, known for its cool climate, lush pine forests, vibrant culture, and iconic tourist spots. As the City of Pines, it offers a memorable mix of nature, heritage, shopping, and dining.

Baguio Track brings these experiences together into one guide: from visitor attractions and historical landmarks to jeepney terminals and travel routes that make your visit easy and enjoyable.

Travel Tips

Key advice for first-time and returning visitors.

🌤️

Pack for cool weather

Baguio can be chilly, especially after sunset. Bring a jacket, comfortable shoes, and layers for changing conditions.

🚌

Use jeepney routes

Jeepneys are the most affordable way to move around. Look for route signs and ask drivers for guidance if needed.

🧭

Choose logical paths

Plan attractions in the same area together to save travel time and enjoy more of the city.

📍

Ask locals for tips

Residents often know the fastest routes, best eateries, and hidden spots that aren’t on every map.
